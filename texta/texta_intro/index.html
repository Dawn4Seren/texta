TEXTA Toolkit for newbies

TEXTA Toolkit contains a set of tools to perform text analytics. Among others it enables fast searches and aggregations, classification of documents and information extraction (the latter two are functional but still at beta status). Following URLs will give a short overview of the different functionalities:

2. Searches

6. Extracting information
